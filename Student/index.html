Why I think CS is a good
career option...

I think it's an all-in-one package (not salary-wise), where you can put your creative as well as logical skills to good use. The range of things that one can do in this field is quite vast, like developing applications, building websites, interpreting complex data using various models, and many more things. The part which I think is the best is that one can learn almost everything on their own and get better with a lot of practice. So if you're confused about whether or not to get into this field, I would strongly suggest you do. And if you have anything tech-related to talk about, feel free to contact me.
